AR Time Capsule

The AR Time Capsule is an innovative concept that merges augmented reality (AR) technology with the nostalgic charm of a traditional time capsule. The idea is to create a digital version of a time capsule that can be accessed and experienced through an AR application.It allows users to create and explore time capsules in a unique augmented reality environment. These capsules can contain memories in the form of images and videos, providing a rich multi-media experience that enhances traditional methods of memory preservation.
